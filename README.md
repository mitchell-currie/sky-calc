# Sky Calc

A 3D sun/moon position calculator built out of frustration with outdated sunrise/moonrise websites and clunky eclipse tables.

## Features

- **3D Earth visualization** - See sun and moon positions from any location on the globe
- **Time travel** - Scrub to any date/time, past or future
- **Accurate calculations** - Real-time sun/moon azimuth, altitude, distance, and phase
- **Sunrise/sunset & moonrise/moonset** - Visual timeline with event markers
- **Eclipse explorer** - Browse past and future solar/lunar eclipses with umbra shadow visualization on Earth's surface
- **Horizon view** - Ground-level perspective with compass heading
- **Telescope zoom** - FOV zoom to track sun or moon
- **City search** - Quick navigation to any location
- **Playback controls** - Animate time forward or backward at variable speeds

No tables. No guessing. Just point, click, and see.
